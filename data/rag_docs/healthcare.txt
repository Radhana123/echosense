HEALTHCARE DOMAIN BENCHMARKS:
- Normal blood glucose: 70-100 mg/dL (fasting)
- Healthy BMI range: 18.5 to 24.9
- High cholesterol: above 200 mg/dL is borderline, 240+ is high risk
- Heart disease risk increases significantly above age 45
- Blood pressure normal: below 120/80 mmHg
- Diabetes diagnosis: fasting glucose above 126 mg/dL
- Obesity defined as BMI above 30
